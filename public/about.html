The company

Propertysearch.com is one of the UK's leading property websites, focussed on helping house-hunters in the middle and upper tiers of the market find their next home with listings advertised by most of the top estate and letting agents across the UK.

At Propertysearch.com everything we do is aimed at providing an effective medium for users looking to buy, sell, rent or let quality properties, both in the UK and overseas. Propertysearch.com has become the leading online destination for property consumers in the UK and abroad looking to find top quality UK homes to buy and/or rent and to discover the UK's leading estate and letting agents.

Propertysearch.com attracts over 5 million visitors a month and advertises properties from over 16,000 estate and letting agent branches, including all the leading national chains. Our overseas property listings include more than 60 countries, including France, Spain, Portugal, Italy, Cyprus, South Africa, Dubai, Australia, Croatia and Bulgaria.

Propertysearch.com is part of Propertysearch Limited which was founded in 2018 and has a highly-experienced management team, led by Founder & CEO, Hakan Akgun.
